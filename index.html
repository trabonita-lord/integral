Overview

This lesson explains how to evaluate definite integrals as net (signed) area between a function and the x-axis.

Signed Area Intuition

Understanding signed area requires recognizing that areas above the x-axis count positively, while areas below it count negatively.

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Identify the function and the interval [a, b].
  2. Calculate the integral using the appropriate methods.
  3. Interpret the results in terms of area.
